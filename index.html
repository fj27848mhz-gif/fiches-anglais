II. Structure en 3 parties

A. Introduction

B. Development (2 ou 3 parts)

point clé

Présenter les opposite views ou les similarities sur 2 ou 3 thèmes communs aux deux docs.

  • chaque partie = un thème commun

structure

III. Reformulations type (les 3 cas)

cas 1 — source précise

On cite l'auteur dans la phrase.
Ex : Selon D. Trump, des mesures de clandestins visent à éradiquer les gangs de la drogue.
→ "According to D. Trump, the mass deportations of undocumented immigrants aim to eradicate drug gangs" (présent).

cas 2 — pas de source précise, info attribuée

Reformulation avec « sont censés... ».
Ex : Selon les rumeurs, des citoyens américains sont censés avoir été mis en détention.
→ "Some Seattle American citizens are rumoured to have been put in detention."

cas 3 — info présentée comme incertaine (conditionnel journalistique)

Utiliser is supposed / is said / is thought / is reputed / is claimed / is presumed / is understood to be / to have been / to have +ed.

11 reformulations modèles
  1. According to / The COP28 is said to have failed.
  2. This oil tycoon is thought to have been pushed to change attitudes under the pressure of oligarchs.
  3. "Trump tariffs" are supposed to be a means to control the population.
  4. He is said to have been very… (according to "Q", the saviour is reputed to eradicate…).
  5. He is understood to hate children. → Il est censé détester les enfants.
  6. He is thought to have been very rich. → Il est censé avoir été…
  7. He is claimed to have legislated in the past.
  8. It is reported to have abandoned his family.
  9. It is presumed to be hiding at the embassy.
  10. It is supposed to have been working very hard at the embassy.
  11. The exchange rate is predicted to decrease on the coming day.
Le verbe "to be" doit être conjugué au présent ("is said") puis "to + V" / "to have + V-ed" selon le tps de l'événement rapporté. Ex : has been put (action passée) vs is hiding (action présente).

  • Faces the island of Manhattan, one of the 5 boroughs making up New York City
